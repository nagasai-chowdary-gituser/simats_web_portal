Portal Services

What You Can Do Inside This Portal

This platform bundles multiple student-focused utilities into a single clean dashboard. From AI-generated capstone reports to simple CGPA tools, everything is designed to remove manual work and give you more time to focus on real learning and projects.

CAPSTONE
AI Capstone Report Generator

Generate SIMATS-style capstone reports automatically based on your project title. The system follows the correct chapter structure, headings, and academic tone, so you get a ready-to-edit Word document in minutes instead of weeks.

Output: DOCX, SIMATS-formatted
ACADEMICS
CGPA & Grade Utilities

Use CGPA calculators and semester-wise helpers to quickly estimate your academic standing, required grades, and target scores – without struggling with manual formulas every time.

Helps with planning and tracking
TRACKING
Attendance Helpers

Simple attendance tools help you understand how many classes you can miss or need to attend to stay within college requirements, so you can plan realistically around exams and projects.

Always aligned with percentage rules
AI TOOLS
Internal AI Utilities

A curated list of AI tools relevant for SIMATS students: content re-writers, research helpers, idea generators, and more – all organized so you can quickly pick the right tool for the right task.

Handpicked & integrated links
FACULTY
Faculty Suggestion Interface

A dedicated panel where faculty can review, comment, approve, or suggest modifications for selected student work, helping both sides align on expectations and improvements.

Bridges student–faculty collaboration
ADMIN
Admin Config & Controls

Admin views allow configuration of services, templates, and settings behind the scenes – such as capstone formats, allowed tools, and portal content – keeping everything under controlled access.

Secure & role-based access

How Students Use These Services

Instead of jumping between multiple apps, messages, and random templates, students can use this single portal as their main academic assistant. A typical flow is: login → choose a tool (Capstone / CGPA / AI tools) → complete the workflow → save or download the output → refine and submit.

A Day-to-Day Student Companion

The goal is not to “replace effort”, but to remove friction. Things like formatting, basic documentation, and repetitive calculations consume mental energy that could be used for genuine problem solving, coding, or research. This portal quietly handles the boring parts.

Faster documentation Cleaner submissions Less manual work
Future-Ready Expansion

The platform is designed to grow: more colleges, more formats, more project types, and deeper AI assistance for research, presentations, and viva preparation – all while preserving academic integrity.

Multi-college support New project types AI-assisted research
