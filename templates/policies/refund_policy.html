Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on Dec 1st 2025

CHITTEM JASWANTH believes in providing the best possible assistance to customers, and follows a clear and fair cancellation policy:

Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made within **1–2 days** of placing the order. However, cancellation may not be possible if the order has already been processed or shipped by the vendor. Cancellations cannot be accepted for **perishable items** such as flowers or food products. Refunds or replacements may be offered if the customer proves that the delivered product was of poor quality. If you receive a **damaged or defective item**, please report it to Customer Service within **1–2 days** of delivery. The vendor must verify and confirm the defect before a refund is issued. If the product does not match your expectations or differs from what was shown on the website, notify us within **1–2 days**. Our Customer Service Team will review the complaint and take appropriate action. Products covered under **manufacturer warranty** must be handled directly with the manufacturer. If a refund is approved by CHITTEM JASWANTH, please allow **1–2 days** for the refund to be processed back to the customer.
